Academy of High Literature




The Academy of High Literature (AHL) is an educational institution dedicated to spreading media and artistic literacy throughout the newer generation. With the rise of AI generation and fall of artistic appreciation among youth, we persevere in our mission of preserving the beauty of human expression.





Hence, our goals are to:

• Guide students to appreciate diverse art forms
• Help students explore art outside of the Western Context
• Move students to explore different forms of art
• Make this world a more beautiful, connected, and understanding places
